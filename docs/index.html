About Dataset

Top Hits Spotify from 2000-2019  [Link]

The dataset contains audio statistics of top 2000 songs on Spotify from 2000 to 2019. There are 18 columns of track data and musical characteristics of the track: artist, name of the track, duration, year, popularity, explicit, danceability, energy, key, loudness, mode (major or minor), speechiness, acousticness, instrumentalness, liveness, valence (musical positiveness), tempo, and genre.
